About the Author

Thomas was raised in Virginia by wonderful parents. Time spent abroad as a kid instilled him with a love for other cultures, foreign foods, and a deep desire to travel the world. Growing up, he most loved his time spent outdoors, at baseball games, and exploring Washington D.C. with his dad. After graduating high school, Thomas attended Brigham Young University (BYU), and then served a 2-year mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Now, Thomas has returned to BYU where he is pursuing a degree in environmental science and sustainability

More than anything else, Thomas wants to explore what it means to be human. He has a strong belief that all people have goodness in them, and that we fulfill our potential as we seek to become better people.
